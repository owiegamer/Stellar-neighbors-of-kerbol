Planets of Kerbol

Here’s a list of all the new planets added by the mod.

The Blurax system

Blurax is a F type star, it is also the closest star to Kerbal making a good target for interstellar missions.

The Opposites

When the opposites were discovered It was thought that they were just 2 rocky binary planets but after more research it was discovered that they both had a atmosphere it was also discovered that one of them had oxygen in its atmosphere which makes Kerbal scientists think it might be habitable. strange signals have also been detected coming from both planets.

The moons of Despher

Despher has 1 moon, Gloob. Gloob is a rockey moon of Desphere, it is small and could be good for refuleing becuse of its low gravity.
